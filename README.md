# Data Science in Economics: AI exposure and the UK Labour Market: Evidence from Employment, Pay and Hiring Demand 
### BEE 2041 Empirical Prokect | Unveristy of Exeter 




---

## Project Overview 

Do  UK occupations with higher AI exposure show different patterns in wages, employment and job demand?


### Research Questions
1. What does AI exposure look like across occupations?
2. How do pay, employment and demand differ across exposure levels?
3. Are those differences statistically associated with exposure?
4. Can non-linear models add extra insight?
5. What kind of labour market types emerge?
